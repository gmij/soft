# Lark (Feishu)

Lark (known as Feishu in China) is an enterprise collaboration and management platform launched by ByteDance. It integrates instant messaging, calendar, online documents, cloud storage, and workspace features to provide efficient collaboration experiences and help teams improve productivity.

## Features

- Instant Messaging: Supports one-on-one chat, group chat, audio and video calls
- Lark Docs: Online collaborative editing of documents, spreadsheets, and mind notes
- Video Conferencing: HD video meetings with real-time transcription
- Calendar and Meeting Rooms: Smart schedule management and meeting room booking
- Lark OKR: Goal management and performance tracking
- Lark Approval: Flexible approval workflow configuration
- Lark Drive: Enterprise-level file storage and sharing
- Bots and Apps: Rich ecosystem of bots and third-party applications

## System Requirements

- Windows 7 or later
- macOS 10.13 or later
- Linux (Ubuntu 18.04 or later)
- iOS 12.0 or later
- Android 6.0 or later

## Installation

1. Visit the official website to download the installer for your system
2. Run the installation program
3. Register and log in with your phone number or email
4. Join an enterprise organization or create a team

## Usage

Lark uses a full-platform synchronization design, supporting use on phones, computers, tablets, and web browsers. Its document features support real-time multi-user collaboration with automatic saving of all changes.

## Why Choose This Software?

**Q: What problem does Lark solve?**

A: Lark is committed to breaking down information silos by providing an all-in-one collaboration platform. It integrates communication, collaboration, and knowledge management, reducing the time cost of switching between different tools. Particularly its online documents and video conferencing features can significantly improve team collaboration efficiency.

**Q: What are its unique features compared to other enterprise collaboration software?**

A: Lark's uniqueness lies in the coherence of product design and fluidity of collaboration. For example, you can directly @colleagues, create meetings, or initiate approvals within documents; the "Minutes" feature in video meetings can transcribe in real-time and automatically generate meeting notes. Lark's design philosophy is to make tools serve people better, rather than making people adapt to tools.

**Q: What types of teams is it suitable for?**

A: Lark is particularly suitable for modern teams that value efficiency and collaboration culture, including internet companies, innovative enterprises, and remote teams. Its advanced product concepts and excellent user experience make it the first choice for teams pursuing efficient collaboration.

**Q: What are the advantages of Lark Docs?**

A: Lark Docs supports real-time multi-user collaborative editing with automatic saving of all changes. It supports mixed content types (text, tables, code blocks, mind maps, etc.) and allows you to @colleagues, insert files, create schedules, and more directly within documents, achieving a true "All in One" collaboration experience.

**Q: How is Lark priced?**

A: Lark offers a free version that includes core features such as instant messaging, document collaboration, and video conferencing. For enterprises that need more advanced features (such as larger storage space, longer meeting duration, and advanced management features), Business or Enterprise editions are available.
